SCORE: 125 – Track points for any game

Make every game night smoother with SCORE: 125 – a clean and flexible scoreboard app for any game. No built-in rules, no restrictions. Just enter player names and start counting.

Download on Google Play

Main Features

Designed to be fast, distraction-free and easy to use.

Universal scoreboard

Use it for board games, golf, card games, darts, quizzes or any custom game.

1–4 players

Each player has a clear, always-visible column.

Flexible rounds

Choose how many rounds you want before starting.

Tap to add +1

Quick scoring with a single tap. Long press to enter custom values.

Automatic totals

No manual calculations – totals update instantly.

Offline & private

Works fully offline. All data is stored only on your device.

Privacy

SCORE: 125 does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

All player names and scores are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded or shared.
